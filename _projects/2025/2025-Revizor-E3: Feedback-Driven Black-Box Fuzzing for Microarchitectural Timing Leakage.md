  Delivering Core Contributions: 1) a Semantic-Aware Coverage-Guided Generation Strategy with anomalous-data andimplicit-dependency labels plus inverse-frequency weighting, boosting instruction coverage from 88.0% to 99.3%; 2) a Violation-Driven Mutation Strategy treating detected violations as leakage templates for structure-preserving expansion, achieving2.7× violation detection over the baseline; and 3) a Dual-Dimensional Filtering Strategy via observability and stability with BSC capacity grading, reducing the false-positive rate from 29.8% to 3.2%. Two previously undocumented timing leaks discovered: MOVNT implicit cache eviction and REP MOVS alignment-dependent microcode paths.
